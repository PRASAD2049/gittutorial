About this position

With the internet widening and linking remote locations of the world like never before, time and space constraints are slowly becoming extraneous. Those looking to work from home, work flexi-hours, telecommute or work part time have more options than ever”

Tools you need to know

Automation, putting Artificial Intelligence to work – these are not just buzz words any more. These are concepts that have been put to work and have profound implications for the work space. The emergence of workplace requirements such as the use of chat-bots & AI has made it necessary to hire new employees, create gig-based contracts with freelancers, or conduct in-house training. In fact, some experts believe that the market disruption may be as big as the shift from agrarian to industrialized societies in the early part of the last century in the West.

Potential Task

  • Think, argue, analyze, strategize and iterate.

  • Be open-minded to suggestions and criticism with an unbreakable spirit.

  • Analyze ‘what’ and not “how” the user does it.

  • Create wireframe specifications with interaction flows.

  • Have an in-depth understanding of clients and users.

  • Apply strategic design thinking to real-world problems.

  • Have an eye for usability, minimalism, and simplicity.
